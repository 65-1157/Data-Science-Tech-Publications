### 2. üìâ Deep Learning Comparison: LSTM vs. GRU vs. MLP (2024)
**Title:** *Neural Networks Forecast Models Comparison for the Solar Energy Generation in Amazon Basin*
**Published in:** IEEE Access.

> **The Scope:** A massive comparative study covering **12 cities** in the Amazon (including Manaus and Labrea) over a **10-year period** (2013-2023).
> **The Analysis:** I benchmarked three Neural Network architectures: **Multi-Layer Perceptron (MLP)**, **Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM)**, and **Gated Recurrent Units (GRU)**.
> **Key Results:** The **LSTM-GRU** models proved most efficient, achieving the lowest error (MAPE **19.2%**) in the city of Labrea.

* **Tech Stack:** Neural Networks (LSTM/GRU), Python, Time Series Forecasting.
* **View Paper:** [üìÑ Download PDF](./Neural_Networks_Forecast_Models_Comparison_for_the_Solar_Energy_Generation_in_Amazon_Basin.pdf)
